Company Overview

Saving Africa With Agriculture

Around 1 in 6 people living in Africa suffer protein deficiency in their diet, BYPORK is an initiative to combat protein deficiency in Africa by pooling resources to farm pigs and giving back 10% of every investment on our platform to the under privileged in our community.

Pigs are rich in protein and offer probably the best business option of all the protein sources given that nearly 80% of the $3Billion pork consumed in West Africa annually is imported.

Business Model
